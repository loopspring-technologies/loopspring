About us

What We Do

LoopSpring is a startup that creates digital tools for communities through websites, mobile apps, and software. Since 2020, we've been sharing our technological knowledge and digital skills with those in need. Through our business, our priority is to use a portion of our profits to help others. To this end, we share our human skills without any financial discrimination. Even the most vulnerable organizations also have the right to digitalization and a digital presence.

The founders therefore want to actively participate in this digital transformation, which could subsequently help many people, the various organizations and the various beneficiaries of these organizations. Digitalization is a major issue these days; a large part of our government is going digital, and thousands of people are still sidelined due to a lack of training and accessibility. Many companies negotiate prices because the economic situation demands it, but negotiating too much and missing out on the digital shift can be fatal to the company's sustainability. We understand the importance and wish to provide our support to structures that serve causes common to our values.

Where We Work

Our roots are in Kanyakumari, in Tamil Nadu, India, and we have ties throughout Europe. However, our active activities are focused close to our headquarters because we aim to be a local partner and supporter of nearby organizations. This allows us to regularly visit the individuals or organizations we support. We also prioritize fieldwork.

Students can count on us to either visit our offices or schedule an intervention at their institutions.

Why We Serve

We believe technology should unite rather than divide. Our driving values include:

  • Putting people first in everything

  • Equal opportunities for everyone

  • Supporting wellbeing, health and spiritual growth

  • Fighting for inclusion and against social exclusion

  • Respecting and caring for our environment

Since 2023, we've dedicated ourselves to educational initiatives that share knowledge with underserved communities, building confidence and skills for an increasingly digital world.

For Whom We Work

We support foundations, charities, and the education system. We take the time to listen and understand before offering our help.

LoopSpring particularly supports students and schools by providing them with resources that enrich their learning paths and develop practical skills. We believe in the importance of developing the potential of each student and ensuring access to education for all, as they are the future of tomorrow. Several charitable organizations (orphanages, centers for the disabled, centers for the sick, centers for the poor) are also lagging behind digitally.

Projects & Actions

Technology for Inclusion

Empowering Disadvantaged Communities Through Access to Digital Education At LoopSpring, we believe technology should be a bridge, not a barrier. Our commitment to social inclusion is reflected in a series of initiatives aimed at democratizing access to digital tools and knowledge:

  • Free training programs for over 250 students and young entrepreneurs each year in Madras, Pondicherry, and Bangalore

  • Local "Digital India" projects bringing essential digital literacy to rural and disadvantaged communities

  • Workshops in schools or at our office to provide basic computer literacy and skills.

These efforts encourage the next generation to understand, learn, and upgrade their skills for future employees. Our programs aim to consider those left behind in the digital age.

Sustainability & Responsible Innovation

Building a better future through ethical technology practices Our approach to digital transformation is rooted in environmental and social responsibility. We seek local partners for our actions and missions. We closely examine our partners' CSR and select accordingly.

  • Deployment of recycled infrastructure and reduction of energy-intensive equipment consumption.

  • Ambitious paper reduction policies and operations that prioritize digitalization across all departments.

  • Awareness campaigns on the sustainable use of technology among our customers and partners.

  • Through these measures, LoopSpring strives to ensure that innovation goes hand in hand with ecological and ethical management.

Community Empowerment Through Technology

Fostering Connections, Growth, and Resilience LoopSpring is proud to collaborate with local communities to co-create effective solutions. We support social impact by:

  • Advocating for equal access to digital tools and services for nonprofits and charities

  • Providing pro bono technology consulting and audits to NGOs working in education, health, and social action

  • Developing free digital solutions so each organization can spread their ideas while simultaneously raising funds to help them continue.

We don't just develop software: we build meaningful partnerships that help change lives.
